About the Magazine

Our Goals

Montverde Ink will publish the best writing and artwork done on campus, whether in the form of photographs, personal essays, poems, paintings, stories, sculptures, plays, and so on. A few jokes and caricatures might even make it into our pages. The issues will occasionally be organized around themes—feel free to suggest one!—but we will always strive to encourage conversations about, and appreciation for, what the students here value most. So if a certain story or image gets you thinking and feeling, whether good or bad, please send in your responses! We're after those creative, engaging conversations that make this school a strong community.
